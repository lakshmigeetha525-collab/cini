About CineBooker

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is CineBooker? ▼
CineBooker is an online movie ticket booking platform that allows you to book tickets for your favorite movies at various theatres in your city. We provide a seamless booking experience with competitive prices and instant confirmations.
Which cities does CineBooker operate in? ▼
Currently, CineBooker operates in Chennai and Coimbatore. We are expanding to more cities soon. Stay tuned for updates on new city launches!
How early can I book tickets? ▼
You can book tickets up to 30 days in advance. This allows you to plan your movie outings well ahead and secure your preferred seats.
Is there a refund policy? ▼
Yes! You can get a full refund if you cancel your booking at least 30 minutes before the show starts. Cancellations within 30 minutes of the show are subject to a processing fee of 10%.
Do you offer group discounts? ▼
Yes! We offer special discounts for group bookings of 10 or more tickets. Please contact our support team to avail these offers.
How do I get my ticket after booking? ▼
Your booking confirmation and ticket details will be sent to your registered email immediately after booking. You can download and print the ticket or show the digital copy on your mobile at the theatre counter.
Can I modify my booking after purchasing? ▼
Yes, you can modify your booking (change date, time, or seats) up to 30 minutes before the show starts. You cannot modify bookings within the 30-minute window.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets (Google Pay, Apple Pay), and UPI payments. All transactions are encrypted and secured with industry-standard security protocols.
Is my personal information safe? ▼
Absolutely! We use SSL encryption to protect all your personal and payment information. Your data is never shared with third parties without your consent.
Do you offer student or senior citizen discounts? ▼
We work with various theatres that offer student and senior citizen discounts. Please check with the specific theatre for available offers during the booking process.
