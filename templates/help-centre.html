üé´ Booking Tickets

How do I book a movie ticket?

Follow these simple steps:

  • Log in to your CineBooker account
  • Select your preferred district and city
  • Choose a theatre and click on your favorite movie
  • Select your preferred date and time
  • Choose your desired seats from the seat map
  • Review and confirm your booking
  • Complete the payment

Which theatres and cities are available?

We currently operate in Chennai and Coimbatore with partnerships with major theatre chains including AGS Cinemas, PVR, KG Cinemas, and Cinepolis. More cities coming soon!

Can I book tickets for a group?

Yes! You can book multiple tickets in one transaction. Simply select the number of tickets you need and choose seats for each person. For bookings of 10 or more tickets, special discounts may apply!

How much in advance can I book?

You can book tickets up to 30 days in advance, allowing you to plan your movie outings well ahead.

üí≥ Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Debit Cards
  • Digital Wallets (Google Pay, Apple Pay)
  • UPI (Unified Payments Interface)
  • Net Banking

Is my payment information secure?

Yes! All transactions are encrypted using SSL technology and processed through PCI DSS compliant payment gateways. Your payment information is never stored on our servers.

Why is there a convenience fee?

The convenience fee covers the cost of payment processing, security, and maintaining our online platform. Different payment methods may have different convenience fee rates.

Do you offer any discounts?

Yes! We offer special discounts for group bookings (10+ tickets). Additionally, partner theatres may have their own special offers and promotions available during checkout.

‚ùå Cancellation & Refunds

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking anytime up to 30 minutes before the show starts. After that, cancellation is not allowed as the show is about to begin or has started.

What is your refund policy?

Refund policy:

  • Cancel 30+ minutes before show: Full refund of ticket price
  • Cancel within 30 minutes of show: 90% refund (10% processing fee)
  • Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days

Can I modify my booking?

Yes! You can modify your booking (change date, time, or seats) up to 30 minutes before the show starts. Simply go to your bookings and click modify.

How long does a refund take?

Refunds are credited to the original payment method within 3-5 business days. During weekends or holidays, processing may take longer.

üë§ Account & Security

How do I create an account?

Click on "Create Account" on the login page, enter your name, email, and password, and you're all set! You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

How do I update my profile?

Go to your account settings (click on your profile name in the header) and edit your personal information. Remember to save your changes!

Is my data safe with CineBooker?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols. Your personal and payment information is never shared with third parties without your consent.

The website is not loading properly

Try these solutions:

  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try a different browser
  • Disable browser extensions
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try again after some time

I'm facing issues during payment

If you experience payment issues:

  • Check if your payment method has sufficient balance
  • Ensure your card is not expired
  • Try a different payment method
  • Contact your bank to check if they've blocked the transaction
  • Contact our support team if issues persist

The app keeps crashing

Try these solutions:

  • Restart the app
  • Check if you have the latest version installed
  • Clear app cache from your device settings
  • Restart your device
  • Reinstall the app if problems persist

I didn't receive a confirmation email

Check your spam or promotions folder. If you still don't find it, contact our support team with your email address and we'll resend the confirmation.

‚ùì Other Questions

Do you have an app?

Yes! CineBooker is available as a web platform and native mobile apps (iOS and Android). Download from your app store for a better mobile experience.

What are your operating hours?

CineBooker is available 24/7 for online bookings. Our customer support team is available from 9 AM to 11 PM (IST) on all days.

Do you offer e-tickets or print tickets?

Both! Your ticket is sent to your email immediately after booking. You can either print it or show the digital copy on your mobile at the theatre counter.

What is the policy for cancellation due to movie cancellation?

If a theatre cancels a show, you'll be notified immediately. You can choose to reschedule for another show or get a full refund, no questions asked.
